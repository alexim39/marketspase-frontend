Important Information

  • Self Transfer to Marketer Wallet: Funds moved to your marketer wallet are locked for in-app use only and cannot be withdrawn.
  • Transfer to Other Promoter: Funds sent to another promoter go to their available balance and are subject to standard withdrawal rules (service fee applies on withdrawal).
  • Instant and Irreversible: All in-app transfers are immediately processed without service fee and cannot be reversed once completed.
  • Available Balance Only: Only your available (non-reserved) promoter balance can be transferred.
