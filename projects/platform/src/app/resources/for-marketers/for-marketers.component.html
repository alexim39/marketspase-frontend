Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common marketer questions

How much does it cost to run a campaign?

add

Campaign costs start from as low as ₦500 and scale based on your target audience size and campaign duration. You only pay when promoters successfully complete your campaign requirements.

What type of content works best?

add

High-quality images and short videos (15-30 seconds) perform best. Content that tells a story, offers value, or creates curiosity typically achieves the highest engagement rates on WhatsApp Status.

How do you verify campaign completion?

add

Our AI-powered verification system analyzes screenshots and proof submitted by promoters. We verify that your content was posted, remained visible for 24 hours, and achieved the required view count.

Can I target specific demographics?

add

Yes! You can target based on age, location, interests, and promoter audience demographics. Our smart matching system ensures your campaign reaches the most relevant audiences.
