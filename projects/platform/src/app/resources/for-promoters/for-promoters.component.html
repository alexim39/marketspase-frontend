Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about promoting

How much can I earn per promotion?

add

You earn ₦200 per promotion. Your earning potential is limitless and depends on you as the promoter, since you can run multiple promotions at the same time. Most promoters earn between ₦1,000 and ₦10,000 per day.

What happens if I don't get 25 views?

add

If your post doesn't reach 25 views within 24 hours, you won't receive payment for that campaign. However, you can still participate in other campaigns and build your audience.

How do I prove I posted the content?

add

You'll need to submit screenshots or a screen recording showing the post on your WhatsApp status, with the view counts, promotion ID, and time and date of the post clearly visible.


Our AI verification system checks embedded watermarks and other promotion metadata. If the submitted proof passes, your promotion will be verified, and payment will be released into your wallet automatically.


In the case of disputes, our support team reviews the submitted proof and makes a final decision.

When do I get paid?

add

Payments are processed automatically into your wallet when promotion is successfully verified. You can withdraw to your bank account or mobile money.

Can I post multiple promotions at once?

add

Yes! You can run multiple promotion simultaneously, as long as each post meets the 25-view requirement. Many promoters run an average of 5-15 promotions at a time to maximize earnings.

Is there any cost to join?

add

No, joining as a promoter is completely free. You only earn money - there are no fees or hidden charges.
